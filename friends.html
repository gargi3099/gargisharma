||Friends||

  • by:Gargi Sharma
  • date:August 4, 2019


In a lifetime, a lot of people you meet,
Some online and some randomly on the street,
In school, in college , even in office,
They are the only ones who actually did notice,
The way you look, the way you walk,
The way you abuse or precisely the way you talk,
They even like your sickface(shitface),
And make fun of you when you try to embrace,
Some are just friends, some meme partners,
Some become best friends and some life partners,
Some are distant but still close to your heart,
Some sitting next to you but still too far,
They disappoint you sometimes but never leave you alone,
They should rather be called 'CLONES',
They may help you achieve your goals,
But they can never stop being assholes!
