||International Women's Day||

  • by:Gargi Sharma
  • date:March 8, 2019


Having all the attention,
Having all the privilege,
Getting so much care,
Having a great image.

Sounds like a dream,
Maybe it really is,
Coz the real world won't let,
You having all of this.

What do you expect,
From a world so mean,
You won't achieve heights,
Unless you are damn keen.

The society won't even let you,
Menstruate in peace,
But it's your calibre,
Which will seize their tease.

Never let your light,
Fade in their darkness,
On this International Women's Day,
I just wish you success and happiness!
