*Courage*

  • by:Gargi Sharma
  • date:November 3, 2018


"Come down Sumit! You'll fall and break your leg!"
shouted his mother continuously.
Turning a deaf ear to all her calls he kept on climbing the tree.

As he reached the topmost branch, he
freed a little bird's wing and set her free.
"Her mother was also worried for her just like you."
said the 5-year old sheepishly.
